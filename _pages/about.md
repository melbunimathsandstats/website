# What is MUMS?
We are a club for all students with an interest in maths.
We run bi-weekly events, like the pizza + board games night, competition maths evenings, and womxn in maths coffee catchups. We're also responsible for the weekly MS Prime newsletter, the annual integration bee, the school maths competition, and various other fun activities. Our club room is in the Peter Hall building room G24. See you soon!
