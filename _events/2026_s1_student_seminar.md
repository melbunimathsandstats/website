We’re launching our weekly Student Seminar Series, a space for undergraduates to explore maths beyond the syllabus. Join our first session with a special launch talk by Dr Arunima Ray, A Tourist’s Guide to Manifolds. The series will then be run by students for students, so come along and get inspired by your peers. Want to present? Whether it’s from class, a side interest, or something you’re curious about, this is your chance to share it. Fill out the EOI now!
